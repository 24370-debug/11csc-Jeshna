Agriculture & Cattle Ranching

  • Expansion of farmland and agriculture
  • Urban development and housing projects
  • Logging and timber harvesting
  • Road construction and infrastructure projects
  • Land clearing for economic activities

Commercial Plantations

    Palm oil, rubber, and other monoculture plantations replace diverse rainforests, destroying ecosystems and reducing biodiversity across Southeast Asia and beyond.

Logging Operations

    Both legal and illegal logging removes valuable hardwood trees for timber, paper, and furniture industries, fragmenting forests and opening areas to further exploitation.
Infrastructure Development
    Roads, dams, mining, and urban expansion fragment forests and provide access to previously untouched wilderness areas, accelerating deforestation rates.
