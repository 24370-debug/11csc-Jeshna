Deforestation is the removal of forests and trees. In New Zealand, forests provide habitats for native wildlife, help control climate, and protect soil and water resources.

Every minute, we lose forest area equivalent to 27 football fields. Together, we can change this story.

This website explains the causes, impacts, and solutions to deforestation in New Zealand.
